People Power Florida

People Power Florida is a poticical action group local to the Central Florida area. The main goal of this organization is to facilitate gaining voter population in minority or BiPoc areas.

Being a part of an organization like this that is helping to balance the political power of the classes and undo years of gerymandering is very fulfilling to me. While I do not nessisarily have a direct passion for voting and civic action I see the importance of this work and want to do my part to help it be done.
